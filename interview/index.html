Cactus Neon Light Signage

On Monday, March 22, 2021, Amaris Brown interviewed Shriya Chunduri about the object Cactus Neon Light Signage, the subject of their focus throughout the Spring 2021 semester of Rutgers Design 1B.

AB: Where did i find the object?

SC: I got the light as a Christmas gift from my parents.

AB: Does it hold any value or story to you?

SC: Yes, the cactus is a symbol of Arizona, which is my birthplace that I miss a lot.

AB: Was it easy to do projects with your objects?

SC: Yes, the signage had different aspects like the cactus, neon light and the neon green that provided unique directions to explore.

AB: Out of all of these assignments, which turned out the best?

SC: I think my poster series turned out the best cause I really the concept and the ability to use type.
